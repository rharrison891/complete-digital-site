What's Changing in Digital TV

Freely — The New Way to Watch Live TV +

Freely is the biggest upgrade to free-to-air UK television in years. Instead of relying on a traditional aerial, Freely delivers live channels over the internet. It combines live TV, catch-up, and on-demand content into one simple interface and is built into many new smart TVs.

  • Live TV streamed over broadband (no aerial required)
  • Built-in access to BBC, ITV, Channel 4, Channel 5 and more
  • Seamless switching between live channels and catch-up apps
  • Cleaner menu design and easier content discovery
Cloud Broadcasting — Freeview Goes Modern +

Much of the UK’s Freeview broadcasting infrastructure is now processed in the cloud. This improves reliability, flexibility, and allows faster updates to channels and regional variations.

  • More reliable signals and fewer outages
  • Faster channel updates and regional variation support
  • Efficient use of broadcasting resources
  • Paves the way for online and hybrid TV delivery
Updated Channel Lists & The Need to Retune +

Freeview regularly updates its channel lineup throughout the year, adding new channels, renaming existing ones, or occasionally removing services. A simple retune ensures your TV has the latest content.

  • New movie and entertainment channels
  • Classic or specialist channels moving to new numbers
  • Temporary or niche channels retired
Possible Reduction of Freeview HD Channels +

Some Freeview HD channels may be reduced as focus shifts toward internet-delivered content. This is gradual and viewers will have plenty of notice.

  • Shift to streaming platforms for HD/UHD content
  • Rising costs of maintaining traditional HD multiplexes
  • Smart TVs and hybrid internet delivery becoming standard
UK Media Bill — Streaming Gets Regulated +

The UK Media Bill ensures streaming platforms follow similar quality and safety standards as traditional broadcasters.

  • Streaming services regulated by Ofcom
  • Rules for quality, safety, and content labelling
  • Better subtitles, signing, and audio description
  • Public service channels remain easy to find
AI-Powered Personalised TV +

AI is being used to personalise recommendations and improve content discovery on smart TVs and streaming platforms.

  • Smarter recommendations based on viewing habits
  • Improved search and content discovery
  • Seamless “continue watching” across devices
  • Customised menus highlight relevant programmes
4K, 8K & AI Upscaling — Future-Proof TV +

Modern TVs use AI upscaling to improve HD/4K content, making 8K TVs useful even now.

  • AI upscaling for sharper images
  • Brighter HDR and improved contrast
  • Better motion handling for sports/action
  • Energy-efficient display panels
