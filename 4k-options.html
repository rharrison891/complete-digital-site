4K Options

There are several ways to watch 4K content, depending on your setup. Here’s a quick overview of your options:

  • Streaming platforms like Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video
  • Ultra HD Blu-ray discs for physical media enthusiasts
  • 4K broadcast channels via satellite, cable, or Freely-enabled devices
  • Game consoles such as PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X

This page will later include details on subscriptions, device compatibility, and recommended viewing setups.
