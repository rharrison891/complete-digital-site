HD & Freeview

What Is Freeview HD? ▸

Freeview HD delivers crystal-clear high-definition channels using your existing TV aerial. It offers sharper picture quality, improved sound, and access to the UK's most popular channels — all without any monthly subscription.

With new hybrid platforms such as Freely, you can now combine traditional broadcasts with online streaming for even more content and reliability.

What You Need for Freeview HD ▸
  • HD-ready TV with a Freeview HD tuner - most modern TVs support this.
  • DVB-T2 reception - required for channels such as BBC One HD, ITV HD, Channel 4 HD.
  • Good aerial signal - a well-positioned and aligned aerial improves performance.
  • Regular retuning - keeps your channel list up to date.
Freely & Hybrid TV Options ▸

Freely brings live TV, catch-up, and on-demand content together using both your internet connection and your aerial. It works seamlessly on supported smart TVs and offers:

  • Live Freeview channels even in weak-signal areas (online fallback).
  • No subscription needed — just connect your TV to Wi-Fi.
  • Simple, modern menus with integrated catch-up apps.
Recommended Setup Tips ▸
  • Position your aerial for the strongest signal from your local transmitter.
  • Use high-quality coaxial cables to reduce interference.
  • Amplifiers can help in weak-signal areas (but aren't always necessary).
  • Enable "Automatic Updates" to keep your channels and apps current.
