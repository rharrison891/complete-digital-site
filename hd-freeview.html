HD & Freeview

Freeview HD offers higher definition broadcasts using existing terrestrial signals. This section provides guidance on getting the most out of HD channels and the new Freely/online options.

  • Check your TV supports Freeview HD or use an HD set-top box
  • Some HD channels require DVB-T2 reception
  • Regular retuning ensures you have the latest channel lineup
  • Hybrid options allow combining terrestrial and internet streams
