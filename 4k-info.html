4K Information

4K (Ultra HD) provides four times the resolution of Full HD, giving sharper images and richer detail. Modern TVs, streaming devices, and consoles increasingly support 4K content.

Highlights:

  • Sharper image and more detail
  • HDR support for better contrast
  • Improved color accuracy and depth
  • Upscaling improves older content
